1.1 Data Collection and Usage

Infinity Pong does not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, or login credentials. However, the application integrates third-party services that may automatically collect certain data.

Collected data may include:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device model and operating system version
  • Gameplay activity and performance data
  • Crash reports and diagnostic information

This data is used for the following purposes:

  • Analytics and improvement of gameplay experience
  • Saving and synchronizing game progress
  • Displaying advertisements
  • Preventing crashes and technical issues

2. Sharing Your Information

The developer does not sell, rent, or manually share personal information. Any data processing or transmission occurs automatically through integrated third-party SDKs.

Your information may be disclosed:

  • As required by law, such as to comply with legal obligations
  • By third-party services according to their own privacy policies

Unity Ads is used to display advertisements within the application.

Personal Data:

Unity Analytics may be used to collect anonymous gameplay statistics and diagnostic data to improve the application.

Collected data may include:

Google Play Services provides APIs and security services required for Android applications.

Online resources:

Google Play Games Services provides achievements, leaderboards, and cloud saves.

Collected information:

Infinity Pong does not operate its own external servers. All data processing is handled by Google and Unity according to their security standards.
